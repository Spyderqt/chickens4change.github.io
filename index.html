Welcome to Chickens for Community

Our project aims to utilize chickens as a unique way to foster a sense of community, promote sustainable living, and support local agriculture in Humphreys County.

Objectives

  • Promote Community Engagement
  • Educational Opportunities
  • Support Local Agriculture
  • Foster Social Connections

How to Get Involved

Join us as a volunteer, attend our workshops, adopt a chicken, or support our project through donations.
